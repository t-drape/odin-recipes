Description

Guacamole is a vibrant and flavorful Mexican dish known for its creamy texture and zesty taste. Typically, it's a thick, green-colored dip or spread made primarily from ripe avocados, mashed to perfection. It often incorporates ingredients like diced tomatoes, onions, cilantro, lime juice, and spices such as salt and pepper, though variations abound.

The resulting mixture is both smooth and chunky, with a harmonious balance of tanginess, freshness, and richness. Guacamole is frequently served as a dip alongside tortilla chips or as a topping for tacos, nachos, burritos, or various Mexican-inspired dishes. Its versatility and irresistible flavor have made it a beloved culinary staple around the world.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
  • 1-2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, (or to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, (or to taste)
Source: ChatGPT

Steps:

  1. Cut the avocados in half. Remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a mixing bowl.
  2. Mash the avocado with a fork until you reach your desired consistency.
  3. Add your complimentary ingredients. (All other ingredients)
  4. Stir the mixture together gently.
  5. Taste your guacamole creation.
  6. Enjoy your guacamole with tortilla chips, or friends!
